Axioms to live by:

Every person is always doing the only thing it can in any given moment in time.

Every decision which every person makes is a function of the entire universe at the time that decision occurs.

Agency (i.e. free will) is an illusion. Therefore, what appears to be a person playing a video game is actually a person watching a movie of a person (itself) playing a videogame.
